The main goal of the Cambs chamber of commerce is to feature services and current events that cater to the Cambridge Business community. The purpose of this site is to connect small and local businesses within the locality and help them drive successful revenues and connections with their customers. This site will also feature a brief historical background of the City of Cambridge and its current business environment.
