Visual Heirarchy

BBC News
bbc.co.uk/news

The Visual Hierarchy principle is observed from how the site used the content image and font-sizes to guide the user on the most important news and the details that follows that specific news item.

Repetition

The Lullaby Trust
lullabytrust.org.uk

The Repetition principle is observed from the recurring color theme of blue, yellow, white, and black text across the logo, content and the image displayed.

Alignment

Hotel Chocolat
hotelchocolat.com/uk

The Alignment principle is observed from how the site created a visually appealing alignment from the main header text, the rolling ad on top of that header text, and down to the hamburger navigation icon and the corresponding content below the call-to-action button.
