TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience of CambsChamber.org is described as below.

Who: Small and local businesses, residents looking into entering the business trade, individuals interested in learning more about the local business community.

Age: 18 and above

Business Size: micro (one-person) to large businesses

Location: Cambridge City and Towns
